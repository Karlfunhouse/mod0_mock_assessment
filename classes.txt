Chair class

attributes:
legs (integer)
materialType (string)
weightLimit (integer)
currentLocation (string)
color (string)
flammable (boolean)

Methods:
changeMaterialType (changes the materialType attribute)
shipChairToUSA (changes currentLocation to USA)
addExtraLeg (increases legs number by 1)
lightChairOnFire (if it burns returns true, if it doesn't then false)
